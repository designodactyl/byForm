Alliance Graphique Internaionale

The AGI was established in 1951 as a means of uniting the world’s leading creative talents in design, advertising, and illustration.

From Saul Bass, to Wim Crouwel, Peter Saville and Paula Scher, the AGI is a who’s who of modern design. A mammoth international effort was required from countless individuals to gather, record and contribute to the design, development and population of this website – the first release in a stage of staggered implementation.
